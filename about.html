Our Story

Ham Construction & Trading Co. Ltd was established in 2004 by a group of visionary engineers with a mission to rebuild and modernize South Sudan's infrastructure. From our first small residential project in Juba to landmark commercial complexes, we have consistently delivered excellence. Our reputation is built on integrity, timely delivery, and superior craftsmanship.

Today, we employ over 60 full-time professionals and collaborate with international partners to bring global standards to local projects.

Mission & Vision
