About Us

Building South Sudan's future with integrity and excellence

Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence Since 2008

Founded in Juba by a team of visionary engineers, Juba Based Construction Co Ltd started as a small contracting firm with a big dream: to rebuild and modernize South Sudan's infrastructure. Over the past 15 years, we have grown into one of the country's most trusted construction companies, delivering iconic projects that stand as testaments to our craftsmanship.

Our journey has been defined by resilience, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to quality. From residential villas to major highway projects, every structure we build carries our promise of durability and elegance.
